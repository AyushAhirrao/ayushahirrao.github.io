MasterKey
"One Password For All Your Passwords..."

© 2022 The Sudo Club

We’ve all been there, trying to login into our account, can't remember the password, clicking the forgot password button and then changing the password making it even more difficult to remember them next time. Although we wish we could keep them stored somewhere but it's not a good idea to keep our important passwords on a file lying around on our computer. They must be stored securely. The MasterKey app is a project by The Sudo Club coded in C programming language, intended to reduce the need of remembering every password you create and promoting its ideology of having one password for all the passwords. And yes, they are stored encrypted.
